NVDA YouTube Downloader

A powerful and accessible NVDA addon to download videos and audio from YouTube directly

Features

  • Format Selection: Download as MP3 (Audio) or MP4 (Video).
  • Quality Control: Choose from various quality options (e.g., 320kbps for audio, 1080p for video).
  • Trimming: Download only a specific part of a video by specifying start and end times (Disabled for playlists).
  • Advanced Playlist Support: Automatically detects playlists, allows you to select specific videos to download, and saves them in a dedicated folder.
  • Smart Detection: Intelligently handles links that contain both video and playlist IDs, giving you the choice.
  • Background Downloads: Downloads continue in the background, allowing you to use NVDA without interruption.
  • Queue System: Add multiple downloads to the queue and manage them easily.
  • Retry Support: Easily retry downloads that were stopped or interrupted.

Usage

  1. Open the Downloader:
    • Press NVDA+Shift+Y (default shortcut).
    • Or go to NVDA Menu -> Tools -> YouTube Downloader...
    • Note: The shortcut can be customized in NVDA Menu -> Preferences -> Input Gestures -> YouTube Downloader.
  2. Enter URL:
    • The addon attempts to automatically grab the URL from your clipboard.
    • If not, paste the YouTube video or playlist link into the "Enter YouTube video link or playlist link" field.
    • Note: If you paste a link to a video inside a playlist, you will be asked if you want to download the whole playlist or just the video.
  3. Select Options:
    • Choose your desired Format (MP3 or MP4).
    • Select the Quality.
    • (Optional) Enter Start and End times (HH:MM:SS) to trim the video (Single videos only).
  4. Manage Downloads:
    • Click "Add to Download Queue".
    • For Playlists: A new window will open listing all videos. Check the ones you want (or "Select All") and click "Download Selected".
    • Monitor progress in the "Active Downloads" list.
    • Use the Stop button to cancel and remove a download.
    • Use the Retry Download button to retry downloads that failed or were interrupted.
